Claims

Note:
The claim module is a robust system designed to handle various types of claims, including technical problems, wrong information, and other related issues. Its purpose is to facilitate the process of submitting and managing claims within a specific context. Users can submit claims by providing essential details, including a subject, description, type, and current state. One of the key features of the claim module is the ability for administrators to respond to the submitted claims. This functionality enables effective communication between the administrators and the users, facilitating the resolution process.
